How To Make Countdown, Clock etc.? expand_more
To create a countdown or clock, navigate to the 'Create' page and fill out the required fields such as name, time zone, and theme. Then click 'Create'.
Creating Countdown is cost? expand_more
Creating a countdown or clock does not cost coins, but it uses your storage allocation. Make sure you have available storage space.
How To Unlock Quest? expand_more
Quests are unlocked automatically as you progress on the platform. Complete previous quests to unlock new ones.
What Daily Rewards Works? expand_more
Daily rewards provide coins and items once per day. Make sure to claim them daily. They reset at midnight UTC.
Account Limits? expand_more
Each account has limits on the number of clocks, counters, and items to prevent abuse. This ensures fair usage for all users.
How To Share Countdown or Clock? expand_more
You can share your countdown or clock via the 'Share' button. It copies a link to your clipboard or lets you share it directly on Discord.
What Happens If Storage Limit Exceeded? expand_more
If your storage usage exceeds the allowed limit, you cannot create new items until you free up space or upgrade your storage.
How To Change Account Settings? expand_more
Go to 'Account Settings' tab to change your password, theme, and other settings. Some features are coming soon.
